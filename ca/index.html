(1) Prove the density of the rational numbers in the real numbers

Statement For any $x, y \in \Reals$ with $x < y$, there exists $r \in \mathbb{Q}$ such that $x < r < y$. Lemma 1 If $x > 0$, there exists $n_x \in \mathbb{N}$ such that $0 < 1/n_x < x$. Lemma 2 If $x > 0$, there exists $n_x \in \mathbb{N}$ such that $n_x - 1 \le x < n_x$. Lemma 3 If $x, y > 0$ with $y - x > 1$, there exists $m \in \mathbb{N}$ such that $x < m < y$.
